Are You A Candidate for Hair Restoration with ARTAS iX?

The ARTAS procedure is designed for the dynamic man. It’s minimally invasive with a quick recovery time, so you won’t need to take more than a day or two away from your work or other activities. Your hair regrows gradually and naturally, and doesn’t require long-term specialized care. And, the precision FUE technique means any scarring will be effectively invisible — so there are no limits on the way you can cut or style your hair once it’s grown in.

The ARTAS Hair Restoration System with ARTAS iX is FDA-cleared for the treatment of male pattern hair loss. The ideal ARTAS candidate has straight, black or brown hair and has remaining healthy hair on their scalp for harvesting. Results take approximately six months to grow in, with best results visible one year after treatment.
